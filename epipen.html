Epi First. Epi Fast.

Use EpiPen as soon as possible

Most deaths or serious injuries from anaphylaxis comes from the delay of an EpiPen injection

Always administer an epipen before calling 911

How to Use an EpiPen

  1. 1. Pull off the blue safety cap
  2. 2. Place the orange tip against the persons outer thigh until it clicks
  3. 3. Wait 10 seconds

If symptoms still persist 5-15 minutes later, give a second dose.

Do not leave a person experiencing Anaphylaxis.

Note: Don't put your thumb on top of the EpiPen when administering it. It will hurt like heck.

Note: It is legal to administer an EpiPen as long as you think that they are experiencing Anaphylaxis
